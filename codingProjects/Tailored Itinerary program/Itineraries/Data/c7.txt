Alexandria Day trip
Take a 3 hour morning drive to Alexandria, founded by Alexander the Great on the Mediterranean coast of Egypt
in 332 B.C. Visit the catacombs and learn about the Greeks who lived in Egypt, then visit the only existing
Roman theater in Egypt. Have lunch in a restaurant overlooking the Mediterranean Sea and the historic
lighthouse of Alexandria. End the day by visiting the Library of Alexandria and the Alexandria National
Museum. Drive back to Cairo.
